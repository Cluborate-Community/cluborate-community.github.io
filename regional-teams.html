CLUBORATE

Regional Teams

The Regional Team is a team of executive leaders who will be managing, coordinating, and leading the establishment and expansion of the Cluborate mission within their specific region. This regional chapter and its team will engage in the process of outreach and management for the clubs in their region to initiate inter-chapter meetings, regional collaborations, and events on the behalf of Cluborate.
