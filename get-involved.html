Club Coalitions @CLUBORATE

As a registered chapter under CLUBORATE, Club Coalitions are aligned with Cluborate's core mission to inspire passionate community youth leaders willing to lead the charge in exposing other youth to more opportunities for leadership, development, and growth, all in benefit of the local community.

Learn More Join

Goals

  • Expand to 10 chapters across different schools
  • Host interchanging activities and event
  • Offer incentives/funding for our chapters

Objectives

The objective of this plan is to engage awareness and impact of the Cluborate community into a wider range of school communities across Canada and the world. While price is a significant factor in marketing, promotion, and chapter funding, increased engagement is a critical factor for expansion.
