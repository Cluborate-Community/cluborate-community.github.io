--- title: EVE one_liner: > A group of passionate individuals that aims to empower youth through knowledge, support, and experiences. --- EVE looks to support other organizations through financial resources and other forms of support, so they, too, can get closer to their feminist-aligned goals. In a society where power - which is often correlated with money - is taught to be finite and zero-sum, we want to teach future generations otherwise. Today, there is an ever growing demand for education on social change and opportunities for future world leaders, and we hope to fulfill this through our unique programs, products, and chapter system.
