--- title: Innovation Hub name: innovation-hub one_liner: > Science debunked: an international online platform for high school students to publish their science related articles --- We are a non profit international science platform providing students with the opportunity to publish their articles on science related topics. Founded by two Polish high school students, Nicola Stanislawska and Maria Rutkowska. We are passionate in science and love writing articles. Our mission is to get our voices heard in the scientific field and to supply students like ourselves with a platform to publish articles on achievements and advancements in the scientific field.
